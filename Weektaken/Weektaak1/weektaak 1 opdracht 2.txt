Receptor for abscisic acid (ABA) required for ABA-mediated responses such 
as stomatal closure and germination inhibition. 
Inhibits the activity of group-A protein phosphatases type 2C (PP2Cs) 
when activated by ABA (PubMed:19407142, PubMed:19624469, PubMed:19769575, 
PubMed:23844015, PubMed:21658606). Can be activated by 
both (-)-ABA and (+)-ABA (PubMed:23844015).

Molecular function: Protein phosphatase inhibitor, Receptor
Biological process: Abscisic acid signaling pathway

Taxonomic identifier: 3702 [NCBI]

DOMAIN
Upon interaction with ABA, the 'latch' and 'gate' loops change in conformation 
leading to a tight dimerization and the creation a surface that enables 
the receptor to dock into and inhibit the PP2C active site.
